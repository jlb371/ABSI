About ABSI

During the COVID-19 pandemic of 2020, many business shut their doors for good. Some, however, did open. One of these small businesses was Mystic Mixtures Potion Bar. It was run out of a small kitchen in Pittsburgh, PA and had only one employee who was also the owner of this new business. The creator of ABSI watched as her significant other learned the ins and out of running a business and the difficulties that come with it. The couple had to spend time learning about how to take professional photos, how a business's social media should run, finding markets to sell at, and the list goes on.
ABSI was created to help those in a similar situation. Starting a business should be fun. ABSI works to keep it that way.
